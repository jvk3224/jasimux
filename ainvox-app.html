Ainvox Mobile App

Role: UX/UI Designer
Timeline: June 2024 â€“ August 2024

Ainvox is a cloud telephony platform developed by Bistux Solutions Pvt Ltd. The app is designed to streamline communication and enhance call management with key features like real-time dashboards, contacts management, call logs, and campaign tracking. My role as a UX/UI Designer involved redesigning the user experience and interface to improve usability and engagement.

Responsibilities

Analyzing existing user flows and interface elements.

Redesigning the login section to simplify user onboarding.

Overhauling the dashboard page to improve data visibility and interactivity.

Enhancing the contacts list page for better user management and search capabilities.

Key Features of the Ainvox App

Dashboard

Real-time analytics and call metrics.

Contacts

Managing contact lists with search and categorization options.

Phone Number

Display and manage phone numbers linked to the account.

Call Logs

View detailed records of incoming, outgoing, and missed calls.

Campaigns

Tools for managing marketing campaigns.

Designs

Design Aesthetic & Approach

Aesthetic: "I focused on a minimalist yet data-rich design that emphasizes clarity and user control. The use of blue tones and clean typography ensures that users have a visually pleasant experience without feeling overwhelmed by information."

Usability: "By focusing on intuitive navigation and clear data presentation, I aimed to make the app user-friendly and efficient for business and personal communication needs."
