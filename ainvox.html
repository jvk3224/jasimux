AINVOX - Cloud Telephony Platform

Role: UX/UI Designer
Timeline: November 2023 - November 2024

AINVOX, a product of Bistux Solutions Pvt Ltd, is a state-of-the-art cloud telephony platform based in Kozhikode, Kerala, India. Designed to revolutionize communication for businesses of all sizes, AINVOX offers intelligent, user-friendly, and scalable solutions to optimize communication strategies, enhance customer engagement, and streamline operations.

As the UX/UI Designer for AINVOX, I was responsible for:

User Research

Understanding user needs and pain points to create intuitive interfaces.

User Testing

Conducting usability tests to ensure smooth and effective user experiences.

Prototyping

Building interactive prototypes to validate design ideas and gather feedback.

UI Design

Creating visually appealing designs that align with user expectations.

Key Features Designed

Call Campaign Management

User-friendly dashboards for scheduling and tracking calls.

Virtual Phone Numbers

Interfaces for managing virtual numbers for businesses.

IVR Builder

Drag-and-drop tools for creating custom IVR workflows.

Lead Management

Simplified CRM integration for managing customer leads.

Browser Calling with MPLS

A seamless browser-based calling experience with high reliability.

Missed Calls & Notifications

Real-time alerts for missed calls and follow-ups.

Toll-Free Numbers

Features enabling easy setup and management.

Call Masking

Ensuring privacy during communications.

Integrations

Seamless compatibility with CRMs and helpdesk tools.
